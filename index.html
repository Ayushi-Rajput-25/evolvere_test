About Us

We the people of

EVOLVERE - SOCIETY OF BIOTECHNOLOGY

Evolvere Society is a collaborative community at NIT Durgapur focused on Biotechnology and research. We aim to promote innovation, critical thinking, and skill-building through projects, events, and knowledge-driven activities.

What We Do

We organize workshops, host conferences, and undertake research-driven projects to foster a culture of scientific exploration and innovation in the field of biotechnology.

Our Vision

To build a platform that empowers individuals to make meaningful contributions to biotechnology through collaboration, research, and a commitment to excellence.
